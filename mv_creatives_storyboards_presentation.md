# High-Fidelity UI Storyboards: Interactive Sequences (V1)

*Presented by the Design Director*

This presentation showcases the final, high-fidelity visual design storyboards and motion sequence frames for **MV Creatives** (Version 1). All transitions are mapped through a 5-step visual state sequence: *Initial → Hover → Active → Transition → Final*.

---

## 1. Hero Headline Reveal & Magnetic CTA

*   **Sequence Progression:**
    1.  *Frame 1 (Initial):* Headline hidden behind thin, carbon spacing lines.
    2.  *Frame 2 (Hover):* Headline slides up vertically through clip-path mask layers.
    3.  *Frame 3 (Active):* Primary CTA button magnetizes toward the user's cursor.
    4.  *Frame 4 (Transition):* Outer glass ring cursor morphs, distorting background elements.
    5.  *Frame 5 (Final):* Full text reveal complete with soft indigo radial aura glow.
