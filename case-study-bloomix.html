Bloomix Platform

Engineering a unified product hub, multi-product interface system, and responsive workflows.

Overview

The Engagement

Bloomix is designed as a multi-product digital hub. The project involved developing a scalable design system, unifying navigation states, and modeling consistent variables across several integrated business utilities.

Client Bloomix
Industry SaaS & Enterprise Utilities
Services UI/UX Design, Design Systems, Responsive Viewports
Design Goal

The Design Goal

The core challenge was visual and logical fragmentation. Because several distinct applications were used across the enterprise ecosystem, users had to adjust to varying spacing, design patterns, and sidebar menus. The goal was to build a unified design system that feels seamless.

Platform Structure

Platform Structure

We mapped the ecosystem out into six distinct modules, all communicating with the central core Bloomix database. This structure ensures users retain active focus as they transition between SaaS utilities.

Unified Bloomix Platform

Central authentication, visual consistency, and a unified platform layout.

StyleFlow

Creative stylesheet editor & formatting utility.

HireWait

Applicant pipelines & candidate profile lists.

Resident Connect

Community board & scheduling matrix.

Saheli Health

Telehealth scheduling & physician logs.

POS

Retail sales logs & customer transactions.

Payroll

Compensation tables & monthly payouts.

Product Modules

Product Modules

Each sub-application is constructed around component modules. Rather than treating each tool as a custom codebase, we built a shared stylesheet framework containing unified button styles, modal borders, and input boxes.

Visual System

Visual Identity

The typography guidelines rely on Outfit for display headings to emphasize visual distinction, while Inter serves body copy layout.

Display Font

Outfit Sans

Used for platform headers, product titles, and menu headings. Provides geometric visual balance.

Accents

Obsidian & Glossy Indigo

Dark base colors mapped against electric indigo accents represent active focus highlights.

UX/UI Showcase

Responsive Screens

Every viewport was designed to scale smoothly from 4k monitors down to small phone screens, keeping dashboards legible at all dimensions.

bloomix.io/dashboard
Design Decisions

Design Decisions

By designing the system inside Figma component libraries and matching them directly to vanilla CSS stylesheet rules, the handoff process was entirely seamless. This eliminated layout translation friction and ensured visual consistency across all product screens.
